Privacy Policy — Fantastech Ops Automation

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Overview

Fantastech Ops Automation ("the app") is a private internal tool used solely by the owner of Fantastech Energy Solution LLC to manage the company's own QuickBooks Online data. The app is not offered to the public, does not accept registrations, and does not process any third-party data.

Data Accessed

The app accesses QuickBooks Online data belonging to Fantastech Energy Solution LLC, including: products and services, chart of accounts, invoices, bills, customers, vendors, and financial reports. Access is authorized via OAuth 2.0 by the company owner.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted session storage on infrastructure operated by Anthropic (Claude). No QuickBooks data is persisted outside the active session. No data is shared with, sold to, or analyzed by any third party.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. Data retrieved from QuickBooks is used solely within the session in which it is requested, by the authorized user (the company owner).

User Rights

The sole user of this app is the company owner. OAuth access can be revoked at any time by visiting qbo.intuit.com → Settings → Manage apps → Disconnect.
